About

Have you ever gotten frustrated at how limited the search provided by Instagram is? If your answer is yes, then Search-a-gram was built with you in mind! This web app provides powerful text and image based Instagram searches, all at the click of a button.

Search-a-gram was written by two students (yes, students!) as part of a course in the NUS School of Computing. Yong Jie is the software engineer who does the complicated work of finding the data you want. This data is then passed to Sarah, who visualises everything as what you see here.

Search-a-gram is still in its alpha stages, so if there's anything you like or want to suggest, feel free to drop us some feedback!
